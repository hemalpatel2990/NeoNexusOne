Modular Level Kit — Prop Selection

Pick the props you want in the starter kit (select all that apply)

1

Wall Segment

Modular wall panel (e.g., 400×400×50). Snap together to build rooms and corridors. The bread-and-butter of level layout.

2

Pillar / Column

Tall vertical element (e.g., 100×100×300). Creates cover, breaks sightlines, looks great when sonar sweeps over it.

3

Ramp / Wedge

Angled surface for verticality. Player can glide up/down. Interesting sonar reflections on the slope.

4

Doorway Frame

Wall segment with a cutout opening. Creates chokepoints and transitions between areas. High tension when you can't see through.

5

Crate / Box

Medium-sized obstacle (e.g., 100×100×100). Scatter for cover or stack for platforms. Simple but essential.

6

Low Barrier / Half-Wall

Waist-height obstacle (e.g., 200×50×150). Blocks movement but not line of sight (or sonar). Creates interesting reveal dynamics.
